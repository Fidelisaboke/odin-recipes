Pasta Salad

A colorful, easy to make, and delicious summer dish.

Rating: 4.5 | Reviews: 100
Prep: 10 mins | Cook: 1 hr | Total: 1 hr 10 mins
Jump to Recipe

Description

Pasta salad is a dish prepared with one or more types of pasta, usually chilled, and most often tossed in a vinegar, oil, or mayonnaise-based dressing. It is typically served as an appetizer, side dish, or a main course.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Dressing
  • Seasoning mix
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Black olives
  • Bell peppers - green, yellow and red

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the pasta.
  2. Whisk the dressing with seasoning mix.
  3. Combine the pasta with vegetables, then toss in the seasoned dressing.
