Popcorn

Simple Popcorn Recipe

Rating: 4.5 Reviews: 100
Prep: 10 mins Cook: 1 hr Total: 1 hr 10 mins
Jump to Recipe

Description

Popcorn is a whole grain food and is high in important nutrients like fiber and antioxidants. It is a healthy snack when prepared with minimal oil and not too much salt or sugar.

Ingredients

    • 3 tablespoons coconut oil or extra virgin olive oil
    • 1/3 cup high quality popcorn kernels
    • 1 tablespoon butter or more to taste, optional
    • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Heat the oil.
  2. Add the popcorn.
  3. Cover the pot, remove the head and count 30 seconds.
  4. Return the pot to the heat.
  5. Once the popping slows to several seconds between pops, remove the pot from the heat.
  6. Remove the lid and dump the popcorn into a bowl.
  7. Melt the butter in the warm pot and drizzle over the popcorn.
  8. Sprinkle with salt.
